Hello!

I was born in Istanbul in 1994 and I live here ever since. Before Marmara University, I was a student at Borsa Istanbul Zeytinburnu Anatolian High School. Currently, I'm a senior computer engineering student at Marmara University and I will be graduating in June 2018. During the fall semester of 2015-2016, I studied at Warsaw University of Technology as an Erasmus Exchange Student.

In the summer of 2016, I've interned with two different companies which are Date My Wardrobe and Ziraat Teknoloji.
Date My Wardrobe is a Boston based startup which brings sharing economy to fashion. I have worked closely with the CEO of Date My Wardrobe on both front and back ends during my internship, and I used mainly Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and MySQL.

Ziraat Teknoloji is an Istanbul based company which gives IT support to Ziraat Bank which is one of largest banks in Turkey. While I was an intern at Ziraat Teknoloji, I implemented a desktop application for determining investment preferences of customers. C# and PL/SQL were the two most commonly used technologies during this period.

In June 2017, I was an intern at Doğan TV Holding. Doğan TV Holding is an Istanbul based company which includes Kanal D, CNN Türk, D-Smart, and many others. During my internship, I have created D-Smart Channel Frequency List page which allows its users to figure out the frequencies of some TV channels. I've also played a significant role on the frontend implementation of sitemap page of D-Smart website. During this period, I worked on the frontend using Bootstrap, HTML, CSS.
